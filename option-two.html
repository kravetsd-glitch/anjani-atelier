— How we work

A four-step engagement, on a 90-day cadence.

We move quickly, but never urgently. Every phase ends with something you can review, sign off, and put live — no two-month "discovery" black holes.

01 Wk 1–2

Immersion

A 3-night stay on property. We listen, walk the gardens, talk to the team — and to the guest, quietly.

02 Wk 3–4

Audit & Strategy

Brand, channel, rate and competitor review distilled into a single working document with 90-day priorities.

03 Wk 5–10

Build & Launch

Identity, photography, web and campaigns — produced and launched in tight, reviewable sprints.

04 Wk 11+

Stewardship

Ongoing campaigns, weekly revenue reporting and quarterly business reviews — for as long as you'll have us.

— FAQ

Things owners usually ask.

Can't see your question? Email hello@pulau.co.

Do you only work with properties in Bali?

+

Primarily, yes. We've taken on a small number of partners in Lombok, Sumba and the Gili islands when the brand fit is right.

How long until we see revenue impact?

+

Performance media moves in week 2. Direct booking shifts compound from month 3. Most partners see year-on-year direct revenue double within two seasons.

Will you replace our existing team?

+

No — we work alongside in-house marketing, F&B and revenue teams. For most groups we operate as a fractional senior layer above an existing junior team.

What's the smallest property you'll work with?

+

We've worked with 4-key private villas and 120-key resorts. The question we care about isn't size — it's ambition and rate position.

Do you take a revenue share?

+

For select partners, yes — typically a reduced retainer plus a performance share on incremental direct revenue. Discussed case by case.
